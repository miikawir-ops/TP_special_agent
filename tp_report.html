Mumbai ITAT Rejects Excess PE Attribution: Revenue Sharing with UK Office Upheld in Cross-Border M&A Deals

The Mumbai ITAT rejected the Revenue's attempt at excess Permanent Establishment (PE) attribution, upholding revenue sharing with a UK office in cross-border M&A deals. The Tribunal ruled that the entire execution revenue could not be attributed to the Indian PE without sufficient evidence, affirming attribution based on actual functions performed by overseas offices.
